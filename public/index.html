The purpose?

This website serves as the PETA for students of TLE 9 - Computer in Lourdes School of Mandaluyong.

This is a requirement for our grade/completion.

This website is a biography for us, explaining our lives, affiliations, photos, hobbies and a single Filipino inspiration of everyone showing our love for the country, appreciation and effort that we put in.

The goal?

Our goal is to show our biography, learn from it, learn basic HTML and CSS along the way and present it in an interesting way that makes it very much worth it.

Our additional goal to is to help everyone learn the value of collaboration and ensuring that our words of ourselves are put out there for everyone to know.
